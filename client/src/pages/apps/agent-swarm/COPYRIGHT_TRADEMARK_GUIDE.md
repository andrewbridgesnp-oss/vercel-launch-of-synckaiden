## ‚Ñ¢Ô∏è **TRADEMARK PROTECTION**

### **What is a Trademark?**
A trademark protects:
- ‚úÖ Brand names (e.g., "Agentic AI Business Swarm")
- ‚úÖ Logos and visual branding
- ‚úÖ Slogans and taglines
- ‚úÖ Product names
- ‚úÖ Service names

## üìã **TRADEMARK REGISTRATION PROCESS**

### **Step 1: Choose What to Trademark**

For your app, you could trademark:

- **Short Name:** "AI Swarm" or "AgenticAI"

4. Search app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play)

---

### **Step 3: File Trademark Application**

#### **üá∫üá∏ United States - USPTO**

**Cost:** $250-$350 per class (one-time filing fee)

**Process:**
1. Go to: https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/apply
2. Create a USPTO account
3. Use the Trademark Electronic Application System (TEAS)
4. Choose "TEAS Plus" ($250) or "TEAS Standard" ($350)
5. Specify the class (software is typically Class 9 or Class 42)

**Timeline:** 8-12 months for approval

**Required Information:**
- Trademark name/logo image
- Description of goods/services
- First date of use (when you started using the name)
- Specimen (screenshot of your app showing the name)
- Applicant information (name, address)

**Classes for Software:**
- **Class 9:** Downloadable software, mobile apps
- **Class 42:** SaaS, cloud services, online platforms
- **Class 35:** Business services, automation services

**Application Tips:**
- File as soon as possible (first to file gets priority)
- Be specific in your description
- Provide clear specimens showing use in commerce
- Consider hiring a trademark attorney ($500-$2000)

---

#### **üá™üá∫ European Union - EUIPO**

**Cost:** ‚Ç¨850 for one class, ‚Ç¨50 for each additional class

**Process:**
1. Go to: https://euipo.europa.eu/
2. Click "Apply for an EU trademark"
3. Complete online application
4. Upload logo/wordmark
5. Select classes
6. Pay fee

**Coverage:** All 27 EU member states with one application

---

#### **üá¨üáß United Kingdom - UK IPO**

**Cost:** ¬£170 for one class (online), ¬£200 (paper)

**Process:**
1. Go to: https://www.gov.uk/how-to-register-a-trade-mark
2. Apply online via the UK IPO portal
3. Covers England, Wales, Scotland, Northern Ireland

---

#### **üåç International - WIPO Madrid System**

**For multi-country protection:**
- File one application through WIPO
- Designate multiple countries (130+ available)
- Cheaper than filing separately in each country
- Website: https://www.wipo.int/madrid/en/

**Cost:** Varies by country (typically $500-$1500 total)

## üìÑ **LEGAL DOCUMENTS YOU NEED**

### **1. Terms of Service (ToS)**
Governs how users can use your app.

**Key Elements:**
- Acceptable use policy
- User responsibilities
- Limitation of liability
- Dispute resolution
- Termination rights

**Templates:**
- Termly: https://termly.io/
- TermsFeed: https://www.termsfeed.com/
- Lawyer (custom): $500-2,000

### **2. Privacy Policy**
Required if you collect any user data.

**Must Include:**
- What data you collect
- How you use it
- Who you share it with
- User rights (GDPR, CCPA)
- Cookie policy

**Templates:**
- iubenda: https://www.iubenda.com/
- PrivacyPolicies.com: https://www.privacypolicies.com/

### **3. End User License Agreement (EULA)**
For software licensing.

**Key Elements:**
- License grant (what users can do)
- Restrictions (what they can't do)
- Intellectual property rights
- Warranty disclaimers
- Update and support terms

### **4. Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)**
For protecting trade secrets with contractors, employees.

**Use When:**
- Hiring developers
- Partnering with businesses
- Discussing with investors
- Sharing proprietary information

**Templates:**
- LawDepot: https://www.lawdepot.com/
- Rocket Lawyer: https://www.rocketlawyer.com/
