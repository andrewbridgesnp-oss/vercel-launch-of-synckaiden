## üí° Important Principles

**1. You Don't Need to Build Everything from Scratch**
- 95% of what you need exists in open-source
- Learn from others, adapt to your needs
- Stand on the shoulders of giants

**2. Start Small**
- Don't try to finish all 51 apps at once
- Build one, test it, deploy it
- Then move to the next

**3. It's Okay to Not Know**
- Every expert was once a beginner
- Search errors on Google/Stack Overflow
- Ask AI assistants (ChatGPT) for help
- Join developer communities

**4. Focus on Progress, Not Perfection**
- Your first implementation won't be perfect
- That's okay! You'll improve over time
- Ship it, then iterate

**5. Learn by Doing**
- Reading is good, building is better
- Type code yourself (don't just copy-paste)
- Break things, fix them, learn
