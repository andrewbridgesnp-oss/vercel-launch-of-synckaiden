## ðŸ” RISK MITIGATION

### Regulatory Risk
**Risk:** Real estate finance is heavily regulated.

**Mitigation:**
- We're educational software, not a lender (no license required)
- Extensive disclaimers on every calculation
- Terms of Service reviewed by counsel (post-launch)
- No guarantees of approval or specific outcomes
- RESPA compliant (no steering to lenders)

### Legal Liability
**Risk:** Users might sue if deal goes bad.

**Mitigation:**
- Liability limitations in Terms of Service
- "Educational tool only" messaging throughout
- E&O insurance ($2-5k/year)
- Conservative financial modeling
- Clear assumptions stated on all calculators

### Technology Risk
**Risk:** Platform downtime or data breach.

**Mitigation:**
- Hosted on Supabase (SOC 2 Type II compliant)
- 99.9% uptime SLA
- Daily automated backups
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Security audit planned before large-scale launch

### Market Risk
**Risk:** Not enough demand for niche product.

**Mitigation:**
- 50,000 FHA 203(k) loans annually = proven market
- Beta testing before full launch
- Free tier to reduce adoption friction
- Pivot to broader renovation loans if needed
